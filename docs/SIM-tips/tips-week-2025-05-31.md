# 🛠️ Tips Week 31 May 2025:

##   Explore the Space of Possibilities.

An advanced prompting technical called Tree of Thought (ToT) allows LLMs to explore different avenues, evaluate different options, and give a final judgment.

This is done by giving in a single prompt several roles that compete for the right solution and a one more as a judge.
